Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Business World Travel, Inc.
Plain-language summary: We collect your name, email and phone number when you submit a quote request. We use it only to respond to your enquiry. We don't sell your data, we don't send unsolicited marketing, and we never share it with third parties except the service providers that help us deliver your quote (listed below).
1. Who We Are

Business World Travel, Inc. ("Business World Travel", "we", "us", "our") operates the website at businessworldtravel.com and provides wholesale business class fare quotation services for transatlantic travel. We are the data controller responsible for your personal information.

You can reach us at any time at [email protected] or by calling +1 (800) 000-0000.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect personal data only when you voluntarily submit a quote request through our website. The information we collect includes:

DataWhy We Collect It
Full name and titleTo address you correctly and identify the lead passenger for the quote
Email addressTo send you your quote and any follow-up correspondence
Phone / WhatsApp numberTo call or message you with your confirmed pricing
Country of residenceTo apply correct fare rules and taxes

We do not collect payment card details, passport numbers, or any sensitive personal data at the quote stage.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your quote request — our agents use your details to research and confirm your fare, then contact you with pricing and booking options.
  • Internal records — we maintain a record of quote requests for quality control and to handle any follow-up questions you may have.
  • Legal compliance — we may retain certain records where required by law or in connection with a transaction you complete with us.

We do not use your data for unsolicited marketing, profiling, or automated decision-making. We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to any third party.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction covered by GDPR or equivalent legislation, our legal basis for processing your personal data is:

  • Contract performance — processing is necessary to respond to your quote request and take steps prior to entering into a contract with you (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • Legitimate interests — maintaining records and improving our service (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR), where this does not override your rights.
  • Consent — where you have explicitly consented by ticking the consent box on the quote request form.
6. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your personal data for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfil the purposes described above:

  • Quote requests that do not result in a booking are retained for 12 months, after which they are securely deleted.
  • Records relating to completed bookings are retained for 7 years in accordance with standard financial record-keeping requirements.

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8 below).

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not currently use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or third-party analytics. If this changes, we will update this policy and our Cookie Policy accordingly and provide you with appropriate notice and choices.

Your browser may store basic session data to support the functionality of the search tool, but this is not used to identify you across sessions or shared with any third party.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have some or all of the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Portability — receive a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

If you are in the EEA or UK and believe we have not handled your data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority (for example, the ICO in the UK at ico.org.uk).

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration or destruction. Quote request data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections and delivered to our agents via secure third-party services.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you have concerns about the security of your data, please contact us directly.

10. International Transfers

Business World Travel Travel Group is based in the United States. If you submit a quote request from the EEA, UK, or other jurisdictions with data transfer restrictions, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and the commitments of our service providers under applicable data transfer frameworks to ensure your data remains protected.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults booking business class travel. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated where reasonably practicable.
