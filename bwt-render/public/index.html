The Honest Explanation

Why can't Google Flights find these fares?

This is the most common question we get, and it deserves a straight answer.

Airlines distribute fares through multiple channels. What you see on their website — and what Google Flights, Expedia, and Kayak show — is the publicly filed tariff. These are the fares anyone can book.

There is a separate distribution channel used by travel agencies and wholesalers — fares that are contracted privately and never appear on consumer booking sites. These are sometimes called net fares, bulk fares, or contracted rates. They represent the same seat on the same flight — just sourced differently.

Our specialists have worked these channels for over a decade. When you request a quote, that's what they're checking.
