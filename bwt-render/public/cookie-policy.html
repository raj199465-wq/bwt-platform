Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Business World Travel, Inc.
Current status: This site does not use tracking or advertising cookies
Plain-language summary: We currently do not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or analytics tools. Your browser may store a small amount of session data to make the search tool work — this is never sent to third parties and disappears when you close your browser tab.
1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites work, remember your preferences, and provide information to site owners.

Not all cookies are the same. Some are essential for a website to function. Others track your behaviour across websites for advertising or analytics purposes. This policy explains which, if any, we use.

2. Cookies We Use

The Business World Travel website currently operates without setting any persistent cookies on your device. Specifically:

Advertising / tracking cookiesNone setWe do not use Facebook Pixel, Google Ads tracking, or any remarketing tools

3. Browser Session Storage

Our flight search tool uses your browser's built-in session storage to temporarily hold your search state (selected airports, dates, passengers) while you are actively using the page. This is different from cookies:

  • It is stored only in your browser's memory for that tab session
  • It is automatically cleared when you close the tab or browser
  • It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • It cannot be used to identify or track you

This is a standard browser feature used solely to make the search experience work smoothly and requires no consent under applicable cookie laws.

4. Third-Party Services

When you submit a quote request, your form data is processed by the following services. These services may set their own cookies in certain configurations, though in our standard integration they operate server-side and do not set cookies on your device:

  • Formspree — processes form submissions by email. See their Privacy Policy.
  • Twilio — delivers SMS notifications to our agents. See their Privacy Policy.
  • Amadeus — provides live flight fare data via API. This is a server-to-server integration and does not interact with your browser. See their Privacy Policy.

Google Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN to render our typography. Google may log the request to their servers as part of normal CDN operation. This does not set cookies on your device. See Google's Privacy Policy.

5. If We Add Cookies in Future

If we introduce analytics, advertising, or other cookies in the future, we will:

  • Update this Cookie Policy before those cookies are activated
  • Add a cookie consent banner that gives you a meaningful choice before non-essential cookies are set
  • Ensure that essential-only cookies can be accepted without accepting optional ones
  • Provide a way to withdraw consent and manage your preferences at any time

We will never introduce advertising cookies that track you across other websites without your explicit consent.

6. Managing Cookies in Your Browser

Even though we do not set cookies, you can control and delete cookies from all websites at the browser level. Here's how to do so in the most common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Please note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of other websites you visit.
